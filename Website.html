Activities

We took part in several school activities, including the 3D Printing Seminar, where we printed 3D cat models. We also created a magazine about Philippine historical sites, participated in the Freshies Salubong welcoming event, and attended the AgentBlazer Champion Workshop. These activities helped us learn new skills, explore history, and get involved in campus life.

We printed pre-designed 3D cat models during the 3D Printing Seminar. These prints helped us understand how the machines work and how designs are brought to life.

We created a magazine showcasing the different places we visited. Each spread highlights the background and historical significance of every location.
